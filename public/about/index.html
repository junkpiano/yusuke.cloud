Hello, internet!

Welcome to my personal website. I am a software engineer in Tokyo, Japan.

My profile as a software engineer is described here. I will keep it updated as my career grows.

This is yet another static website built on top of hugo, but is leveraged by a bunch of tech stacks to streamline my blogging. See more details at this post.

Don’t you think that what’s the hardest thing is to understand yourself? Since I often get lost and annoyed, I need to look back and retrospect my life. If I find something useful for you as well, let me share it with you. Feel free to come and enjoy.

Have fun!

PS. I am micro blogging. I am yet another blog lover.
